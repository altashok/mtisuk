Services

 

Network Planning, Design & Optimisation

MTIS provides Network Planning, Design & Optimisation services to its customers, right from RF & Transmission engineering to Fixed and Core Network Engineering Design in the Wireline and Wireless domain. MTIS use world-class tools and disciplined design processes to provide end-to-end design solutions.

 

Network Deployment & Consolidation

MTIS offers a comprehensive suite of network deployment services that support every phase of the network deployment/consolidation lifecycle. The offerings include active and passive infrastructure services for wireline & wireless domains including GSM, CDMA, LTE, microwave & optical technologies.

 

Network Operations & Maintenance

MTIS' Network Operations & Maintenance service portfolio enables Network Operators & OEM’s to focus on the core of their business in marketing, brand building and value creation while ensuring effective network operations and maintenance activities thus yielding significant reduction in OPEX

 

Project Management

MTIS has a wealth of experience in project management and has been involved in the majority of large scale UK network rollouts in the last decade. We have provided everything from small bespoke projects to end to end, long term, network evolution programme delivery.

 

Customer Experience and Network Testing

MTIS work with several different tools and system companies to ensure we offer the best possible service based on the scope of the project. By not tying ourselves to specific products we are able to tailor our services to meet the exact requirements of our customers and their networks.

 

Technical Support

MTIS engineers provide 1st, 2nd and 3rd line support across multiple networks and technologies. We have a vast amount of experience supporting in life equipment from several different vendors. Our engineers provide services remotely and onsite to ensure that network issues are resolved quickly and effectively.

 

MTIS UK Limited provide services to private business and government organisations throughout UK, and specialize in the rollout of Telecommunications, Deployment & Intergeneration, Operation & Maintenance, Testing & Optimization, RAN Support and much more.
